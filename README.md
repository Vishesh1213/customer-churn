# 📊 Customer Churn Prediction

## 🚀 Overview

Customer churn is a major challenge for telecom companies, as losing existing customers can significantly impact revenue and business growth. This project leverages Machine Learning techniques to predict whether a customer is likely to leave a telecom service provider based on their account information, subscribed services, and usage patterns.

The goal is to help businesses identify at-risk customers and take proactive steps to improve customer retention.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Customer retention is essential for long-term business success. Telecom companies often struggle to identify customers who are likely to discontinue their services.

This project aims to develop a predictive model that can accurately classify customers as:

* **Churn = Yes** → Customer is likely to leave
* **Churn = No** → Customer is likely to stay

By predicting churn in advance, businesses can implement targeted retention strategies and improve customer satisfaction.

---

## 📂 Dataset Information

This project uses the **Telco Customer Churn Dataset**, which contains customer demographics, service subscriptions, billing information, and account-related details.

### Key Features

* Gender
* Senior Citizen
* Partner & Dependents
* Tenure
* Phone Service
* Internet Service
* Online Security
* Tech Support
* Contract Type
* Payment Method
* Monthly Charges
* Total Charges

### 🎯 Target Variable

**Churn Value**

* `1` → Customer Churned
* `0` → Customer Retained

---

## 🔄 Project Workflow

1. Data Collection & Understanding

4. Feature Engineering

7. Churn Prediction

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* Customers with month-to-month contracts show higher churn rates.
* Short-tenure customers are more likely to leave.
* Higher monthly charges can contribute to churn.
* Value-added services such as technical support may improve customer retention.
